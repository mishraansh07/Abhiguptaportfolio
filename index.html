Cosmic Queries

Your questions about the astrological journey, answered.

Vedic Astrology, or Jyotish, is the ancient Indian science of light. It analyzes the positions of celestial bodies at your time of birth to understand your unique karmic blueprint, personality, life events, and spiritual path.

For an accurate reading, I require your exact date of birth, time of birth, and place of birth (city and country). The more precise the birth time, the more detailed and accurate the chart analysis will be.

While an exact time is ideal, a close approximation can still provide valuable insights, especially for the placement of slower-moving planets. If the time is completely unknown, we can use other techniques, though some specific predictions might be limited.
